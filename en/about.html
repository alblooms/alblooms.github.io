Our Vision

Alblooms, a startup incubated within the Image and Visual Representation Laboratory at EPFL and supported by the EPFL Startup Launchpad, addresses a modern challenge: managing the immense volume of photos we capture daily. Recognizing that everyone today is a photographer, Alblooms provides a platform that simplifies the process of organizing and sharing photos among friends and family, ensuring that your precious memories are preserved in one cohesive collection.

Our Journey

In 2023, Alblooms was awarded a CHF 30,000 Ignition Grant from the EPFL Startup Launchpad. This grant has been instrumental in enhancing our app's encryption, stability, and data privacy features. Our goal is to launch an Android app in open beta soon, paving the way for broader accessibility and user feedback.
